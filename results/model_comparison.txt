Medical Specialty Classification - Model Results
================================================

Dataset    : MTSamples (Kaggle)
Specialties: 8 true medical specialties
Samples    : 3823

Model Results:
--------------
Baseline (TF-IDF + Logistic Regression) : 38.00%
DistilBERT (distilbert-base-uncased)    : 63.55%
ClinicalBERT (medicalai/ClinicalBERT)   : 68.72%

Key Findings:
-------------
- Removing format-based specialties (Consult, SOAP, General Medicine)
  improved model performance significantly
- ClinicalBERT marginally outperforms DistilBERT on medical text
- Dataset size (2823 samples) is the primary ceiling for accuracy
- Radiology and Cardiovascular show highest confidence predictions
